What is soarFitness.app?

In a word…awesome. It is a body composition assessment tool designed for personal trainers, but accessible to both novice & professional fitness enthusiast.

It simplifies body composition assessment by cutting out time spend with a calculator to let you focus your time on goal setting & dream achieving.

How do I use the app?

After you log in your client's info, you can have a complete body composition assessments in a few short clicks. Composition assessments include:

Max Heart Rate

Target Heart Rate based on training zone

BMI

Body Fat Percentage

Total Body Fat Mass

Total Body Lean Mass

Ideal body weight based on body fat percentage goal
